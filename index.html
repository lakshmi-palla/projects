About School

The Montessori School, Lajapathyroy Peta, Palakol is running with 1000 students strength and around 100 committed staff members under the direct supervision of eminent management obtaining Pratibha Awards. The promotion of discipline in a caring environment assists the students to develop ethical,moral&alround development.

The Montessori School conducting IIT foundation classes for 6th to 10th class students,to make students competitive. The Montessori School is the Lowest Fee pattern Government Recognized School which provides Quality Education with all amenities and facilities in Palakol and running with a holy cause of creating tomorrow's bright society.
